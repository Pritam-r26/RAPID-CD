SCIENTIFIC DISCLAIMER
---------------------
RAPID-CD is a research tool designed to assist in the preliminary processing and
visualization of circular dichroism spectroscopy data. All thermodynamic
parameters (ΔG, ΔΔG, Tm) produced by this software are model-dependent apparent
quantities and should be interpreted as comparative rather than absolute
thermodynamic parameters. The software does not replace expert scientific
judgement. Users are responsible for validating results against appropriate
controls and for ensuring correct interpretation of their experimental data.

The secondary structure estimations produced by the internal NNLS screening
are qualitative indicators only and should not be used as the primary method
for secondary structure determination in published research without corroboration
from established deconvolution servers (BeStSel, DichroWeb) or complementary
methods.
